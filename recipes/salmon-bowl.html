Salmon Rice Bowl

Description

This recipe is for a quick salmon bowl which contains rice, salmon, soy sauce and everything bagel seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Rice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Crushed chilli flakes
  • Sesame seasoning for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the salmon in the air fryer and start cooking the rice.
  2. In a pan combine soy sauce, brown sugar, chilli flakes, salt and pepper.
  3. Heat over medium heat until desired consistency.
  4. Add rice to bowl. Add salmon on top. Drizzle with sauce.
  5. Garnish and enjoy.
