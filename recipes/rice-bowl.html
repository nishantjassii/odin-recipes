Korean Beef Rice Bowl

Description

This recipe is of a Korean rice bowl which contains beef. It is quite a simple recipe and it only takes a few ingredients. You can make this very quickly and I may just make it for dinner tonight.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Rice (brown or white)
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds/seasoning for garnish

Steps

  1. Brown the beef in a pan.
  2. Cook the rice while the beef is browning.
  3. Add ginger and garlic, salt and pepper, and soy sauce to the beef.
  4. Let the garlic and ginger cook into the beef. Add brown sugar.
  5. Once cooked, add the beef to the rice.
  6. Garnish with the sesame seasoning and enjoy.
