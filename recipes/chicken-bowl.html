Chicken Rice Bowl

Description

This is a recipe for a chicken rice bowl. It is similar to the other recipes.

The chicken bowl contains chicken, rice, and garnish along with a soy based sauce on top.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
    • Rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Brown sugar
  • Chilli flakes and sesame seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook chicken thighs in an air fryer. Cook rice separately.
  2. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, and seasonings to a pan and heat until desired consistency.
  3. Add chicken to rice in a bowl.
  4. Garnish and enjoy.
